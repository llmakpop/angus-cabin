# notes - angus cabin app



## build application to manage family lake cabin



###### About the Cabin



cabin is a shared space open in the summer months in rural Minnesota on Lake Miltona, has been in the family since the 1970s. Currently the 4 living Angus siblings and Grandpa Angus go there as well as ass their children and grand children as well as spouses and friends



##### What we're looking for



Primary purpose is a way to see who will be there and when as a shared calendar, but want to incorporate more information and utility.



Types of stays:

\- multiple families there for a long weekend

\- 4th of July (usually most attended time of the summer)

\- single family

\- 1 family + their friends and/or extended family

\- 1 family wants to be there solo



###### Basic requirements

* login/users
* calendar



##### About the Space

##### Booking



###### When a Stay takes place:

* if a single family is wanting to "book out" the full cabin for a period of time, just need to know dates/times
* if multiple families are going to be around, more information is needed



###### Single-family booking:

**TYPE 1:** single family/individual book-out

* single family books for a period of time
* log dates/times

**TYPE 2:** single family/individual book-out with guests

* single family books out for a period of time with guests
* log dates/times \& guests (guests do not need users)



###### Multi-family booking:

* know who, when, and where they are sleeping
* know if pets are attending
* know if children are attending
* know what food they're bringing and/or if they're planning any meals for the group
